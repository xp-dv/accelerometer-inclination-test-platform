Sets the system\textquotesingle{}s active setpoint and transitions to the movement state. 

This function updates the system\textquotesingle{}s active setpoint with the target X and Y coordinates and the desired movement speed, after validating that the inputs are within the allowed ranges. It also transitions the system to the state required for executing the movement.

{\em y} & The target position for the Y-\/axis in degrees. Must be within \mbox{[}ANGLE\+\_\+\+INPUT\+\_\+\+MIN, ANGLE\+\_\+\+INPUT\+\_\+\+MAX\mbox{]}. \\
\hline
{\em speed} & The speed of the movement. Must be within \mbox{[}SPEED\+\_\+\+MIN, SPEED\+\_\+\+MAX\mbox{]}.\\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}

Stops all active platform operations and transitions to the idle state. 

This function halts PWM signals for the specified channels to immediately stop power delivery to platforms or servos. It transitions the system to {\ttfamily IDLE\+\_\+\+STATE} to indicate that operations are no longer active.
